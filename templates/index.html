HOW

WORKS

Nexus Eval is an AI-powered automated answer sheet evaluation system combining computer vision, natural language processing, and handwriting analysis to deliver objective, consistent, and fast assessments at scale.

01
🖼

OCR EXTRACTION

Tesseract OCR reads handwritten sheets after preprocessing — grayscale, Gaussian blur, Otsu thresholding — for maximum text accuracy.

02
🧠

BERT SEMANTICS

SentenceTransformer (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) encodes student and reference answers. Cosine similarity measures conceptual alignment.

03
🔑

KEYWORD CHECK

Key topic words are verified against the student answer. Missing keywords reduce the score; found ones confirm topic coverage.

04
📊

PRESENTATION AI

OpenCV analyzes Laplacian variance (clarity), ink density, and line alignment — scoring handwriting presentation quality.

FORMULA
Content Score  =  (0.60 × Semantic) + (0.25 × Keyword) + (0.15 × Length)  ×  MaxMarks
Presentation   =  (0.40 × Clarity)  + (0.30 × InkDensity) + (0.30 × Alignment)  ×  2
Total Score    =  Content Score + Presentation Score

ℹ How It Works

🖼️
Step 1: OCR

Tesseract OCR reads the handwritten answer sheet image and extracts text after preprocessing (grayscale, Gaussian blur, Otsu thresholding).

🧠
Step 2: BERT Semantics

SentenceTransformer (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) encodes both the student and reference answers. Cosine similarity measures how close the meanings are.

🔑
Step 3: Keyword Check

Key topic words are checked against the student answer. Missing keywords reduce the score. Found keywords confirm topic coverage.

📊
Step 4: Presentation

OpenCV analyzes the image for clarity (Laplacian variance), ink density, and line alignment — giving a handwriting quality bonus.

Scoring Formula (from notebook): 
Content Score  = (0.60 × Semantic) + (0.25 × Keyword) + (0.15 × Length) × MaxMarks
Presentation   = (0.40 × Clarity) + (0.30 × InkDensity) + (0.30 × Alignment) × 2
Total Score    = Content Score + Presentation Score
